FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

How to search a specific destination?

You can use the search bar at the top of the Destinations page to find specific locations. Simply type in the name of the destination you're looking for, and our system will show you all relevant results including popular attractions, accommodations, and travel guides.

Are the information in the website accurate?

Yes! All the information on Explore is carefully researched and regularly updated to ensure accuracy and reliability. We gather details from trusted sources, official tourism boards, and verified local insights to give you the most authentic travel guides possible. However, since travel conditions and local guidelines may change over time, we recommend checking with local authorities or official websites for the latest updates before your trip.

Can I trust the reviews on Explore?

Absolutely! We verify all reviews to ensure they come from genuine travelers who have actually visited the destinations. Our moderation team checks each review for authenticity and removes any suspicious or fraudulent content to maintain the integrity of our community feedback.

Can I contribute articles or travel photos to Explore?

Yes, we welcome contributions from passionate travelers! You can submit your travel stories, tips, and photos through our User section. Simply create an account, and you'll be able to share your experiences with our community. All submissions go through a review process to ensure quality and relevance.

Is Explore free to use?

Yes! Explore is completely free to use. You can browse destinations, read articles, view photos, and access all travel guides without any subscription fees or hidden charges. Our mission is to make travel planning accessible to everyone.

How often is the information updated?

We update our content regularly to keep information current and relevant. Major destinations and popular articles are reviewed monthly, while breaking news or significant changes are updated immediately. Our team continuously monitors travel advisories and local updates to ensure you have the most recent information.
